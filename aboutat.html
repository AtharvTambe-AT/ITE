SSC- Rayat

Rayat Shikshan Sanstha is a large educational organization founded in 1919 by social reformer Dr. Karmaveer Bhaurao Patil in Satara, Maharashtra. Its mission is to provide mass education, particularly to the underprivileged, and operates under the motto "Education through self-help".

Founder: Dr. Karmaveer Bhaurao Patil, a social reformer and educationist.

Founded: 1919.

Headquarters: Satara, Maharashtra.

Motto: "Education through self-help".

Mission: To provide quality education to the masses, especially the poor, weak, and marginalized.

Logo: A banyan tree, symbolizing its wide reach.

HSC- PVP College

Padmashri Vikhe Patil (PVP) College, located in Pravaranagar (Loni), is a well-established Arts, Science & Commerce college under the Pravara Rural Education Society (PRES) offering UG/PG programs, known for its focus on student welfare, research, good infrastructure (labs, digital library), and holistic development with initiatives like the "Earn & Learn Scheme". It's part of a larger educational complex in Loni, which also includes engineering and polytechnic institutions.

MHT CET (Maharashtra Common Entrance Test) is a state-level entrance exam conducted by the Maharashtra CET Cell for admission to undergraduate engineering, pharmacy, and agriculture programs in Maharashtra. The exam assesses skills in Physics, Chemistry, and either Mathematics or Biology, depending on the chosen stream. To be eligible, candidates must have passed their 12th grade with the required subjects and be an Indian national. The exam is conducted annually, and the results are used for the CAP counseling process to allocate seats
